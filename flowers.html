A Summary of Flowers
A bit easier to define then trees, flowers have five to six major parts:
  • The Petal This is the part that really gives a flower it's shape and colour, and is usually brightly coloured to attract critters to polinate it!
  • The Sepal
    • These are the leafy parts right beneath the petals, and they're meant to protect them before they blossom.
  • The Peduncle This is the stem/stalk of the flower.
  • Receptacle This is that thick part at the bottom, holding the major orangs of the flower.
  • Pistil The female organ of the flower. It has a stigma, style, ovary, and ovules.
  • Stamen The male organ of the flower. It has an anther and filament.
Not all flowers can reproduce on their own, with both a male and female organ inside -- some have only male or female organs, and so need a flower of the other organs so they can reproduce!
