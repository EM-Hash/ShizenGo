Common Primrose

Primula Vulgaris

Credit: Britannica

A flower with a lot of variety, there are actually between 490 and 600 species! Just about everything about this flower can vary, from colours that can range from red, pink, purple, blue, white, and yellow, to a height that, whle for most species is between 10 and 20 inches, can range from 2 inches to four feet!
While the flower shown is the Common Primrose, different species are native to different regions, so may be associated with specific meanings. Thus, the country may have a the primrose species specified next to it.
