Austrian Pine Tree - Conifer Tree

Pinus Nigra

Credit: Arbor Day Foundation

There are many variations on the pine tree, but one thing stays true: They're resilient, able to keep their needles green even through winter. The pine tree holds pinecones, which actually hold the pine tree's seeds, able to use its "scales" to keep the seeds inside safe from cold weather. (Scientific America)

This table has meanings for a few different pine trees. All of this kind have the genus pinus, but different trees can vary in appearance, such as how high up the needs appear, and even the shape of the trunk.
